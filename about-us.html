About Us

Looking for a high-quality aged care facility in Mornington? Look no further than CraigCare Mornington! Situated in the serene Mornington Peninsula, this welcoming and supportive community provides a place where residents can truly feel at home.

One of the standout features of CraigCare Mornington is its beautiful location. Surrounded by the tranquility of Mornington, residents have access to breathtaking gardens and outdoor spaces, allowing them to enjoy the natural beauty of their surroundings. And with the beach, shops, and restaurants conveniently close by, there's always something exciting to explore.

When it comes to accommodations, CraigCare Mornington offers spacious and comfortable living options. Whether you prefer the privacy of a single room or enjoy the company of a companion, their variety of accommodation choices cater to your personal preferences and needs.

CraigCare Mornington goes above and beyond by providing a wide range of services to meet the needs of its residents. From 24-hour nursing care and personal assistance to delicious meals, housekeeping, and laundry services, every aspect of daily life is taken care of. The dedicated staff, including experienced nurses, caregivers, and support staff, are committed to providing individualized care that prioritizes the well-being and comfort of each resident.

But it's not just about the physical care – CraigCare Mornington fosters a welcoming and supportive community. Residents have access to a variety of social activities and programs designed to keep them active, engaged, and connected. They also have opportunities to participate in outings and events within the wider community.

Choose CraigCare Mornington for a high-quality aged care facility that offers a welcoming environment, exceptional services, and dedicated staff. Contact them today to learn more and experience the difference of this remarkable community.
